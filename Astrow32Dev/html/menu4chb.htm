Heliocentric Report, (Compile Menu).

This Compiles a Heliocentric Report. This tells you the Heliocentric angles made by The planets based on the date in the Birth Information Screen. Normal Astrology is based on ancient beliefs, which believed that the earth is at the center of the universe. A Heliocentric chart takes the proper assumption that the planets circle the Sun. A Heliocentric report is used in conjunction with a natal report.
